Our Story

The journey of Madre Tierra Cigars

Our Heritage Begins

Madre Tierra is a family-owned and operated brand of fine cigars, all hand made in Tamboril, Dominican Republic. Our blended Cigars compliment the finest tobacco grown in Ecuador and Dominican Republic and we own the entire process from "seed to smoke".

Madre Tierra has been blending cigars since 1982 and just recently owners Juanna De La Cruz, John Deans and Jacob Senior introduced the cigars to the U.S.

Our Craft & Collection

Madre Tierra currently produces 10-blends of Toro (6x52) sized cigars: Connecticut, Rosado, Habano, San Andres, Candela, La Fuma, Pennsylvania Broadleaf, Cameroon, Sumatra, and Corojo.

The cigar gives of a distinct flavor profile yet much more approachable than other cigars from the region.

Our Market Triumph

Madre Tierra made it's first major debut during a "cut-n-light* event for Smoke Rings-72, a local cigar lounge located in Merrit Island FL. www.smokering72.com

During the event the Habano flew off the shelf faster than it could be restocked. The trend continues to this day with the Habano (red-label) quickly becoming their highest rated and #1 Seller in the U.S. (next to the San Andreas).

Dominican Excellence

Almost half of all the worlds finest cigars originate from Dominican Republic and Madre Tierra is certainly living up to the standard of producing quality cigars full of robust Dominican flavors with hints of cedar, spices, nuts, coffee and even licorice as you work your way to the nub.

Premium hand rolled cigars are and will always be about the tobacco and Madre Tierra takes pride in owning the entire cultivating process from seed to smoke, so you can be guaranteed the very best every time you smoke it…..enjoy!
